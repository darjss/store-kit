# Plugged catalog and search references

## Assets

- `catalog-desktop.png` — 1440 × 960 desktop catalog and filter composition.
- `catalog-mobile.png` — 390 × 843 mobile catalog recomposition.
- `search-mobile.png` — 390 × 843 focused search overlay with results.

## Visual decisions

- The selected v5 cold warehouse-night system carries through as ink-black structure, deep petrol fields, dirty-silver paper, photocopy wear, torn edges, and restrained electric-cyan registration marks.
- Product photography and long white utility strips keep names, prices, stock, and actions readable inside the intentionally skewed editorial layout.
- Navigation uses the broad `ACCESSORIES` destination alongside primary `IEM`
  and `DAC` categories. Accessories may eventually include source-backed
  eartips, replacement cables, cases or pouches, plug adapters, ear hooks or
  clips, filters or nozzles, and cleaning tools, but the references do not
  claim those products are in stock. Eartips and cables are not promoted as
  categories or merchandise.
- Mobile alternates product imagery and price/action strips vertically instead of shrinking the desktop rows.

## Interaction decisions

- Catalog references preserve query search, use-case filtering, a secondary type/brand filter, stock state, and attached product actions.
- The mobile filter ribbon communicates horizontal overflow while the type/brand control remains full-width and thumb-friendly.
- Search is a full-screen focused state with a visible query, result count, two tappable results, close action, and keyboard hints.
